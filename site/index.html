About

Deaf‑first architect with business receipts and code in production

My path runs through insurance, tax, real estate, and compliance — then straight into AI‑powered, Deaf‑first platforms. I’ve filed returns, closed deals, taught financial literacy, and sat on Deaf chamber boards. Platforms

Magi is the orchestration layer. These are the spells.

  • DeafAUTH
    Deaf‑first identity and authentication — a passport, accessibility profile, and compliance key for Deaf professionals moving through MBTQ services, VR4Deaf, 360Magicians, and partner platforms.
    Accessibility, trust, Web2 + Web3 logins, sign‑language–aware flows.
  • PinkSync
    An accessibility broker that coordinates speech, text, and sign language services so Deaf users get the right support at the right moment instead of being an afterthought.
